The Life Cycle of a Butterfly

Explore the beautiful transformation of butterflies through their fascinating life cycle.

Stages of the Life Cycle

  1. Egg
    • - Laid on leaves
    • - Takes a few days to hatch
  2. Larva (Caterpillar)
    • - Eats leaves constantly
    • - Grows quickly
  3. Pupa (Chrysalis)
    • - Transformation occurs inside
    • - No eating during this stage
  4. Adult Butterfly
    • - Emerges fully formed
    • - Ready to mate and continue the cycle
